About Me

                  ज्ञानामृत was developed by a 'Mr. Nitin Yadav' who wanted to create a digital haven for Marathi book lovers worldwide. His journey began with the vision of preserving and celebrating Marathi literary treasures in a digital format. Over the years, He has diligently worked to curate an extensive collection of Marathi books, ranging from commonly readen books as Autobiography, historical, Novels etc and Educational Books also. His this library helps reader to read book anywhere, anytime 24/7.

First Online Marathi Book Library for Free

This library is developed for who loves reading books in marathi or english. Now, with this library reader can read books from anywhere and anytime, 24/7.
